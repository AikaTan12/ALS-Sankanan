ALS provides a second chance for individuals who were unable to complete their formal education.

ALS AIM:

Enhanced Learning Resources for Alternative Education:

The Department of Education (DepEd) has acquired over 87 million learning modules and 74,492 tablets under the Flexible Learning Options (FLO) fund to support around 300,000 learners in 16 regions, especially in high- and medium-risk areas. This initiative ensures that students in the Alternative Learning System have the tools they need to continue their education.

Specialized Training for ALS Implementers:

Nearly 300 ALS implementers have undergone specialized training, supported by UNESCO, to enhance the effective use of these learning resources. This initiative aims to improve the delivery of ALS programs and ensure that educators are well-equipped to support learners in diverse settings.
